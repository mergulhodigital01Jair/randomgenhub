Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RandomGen Hub handles information when you visit https://randomgenhub.net/.

The short version

The RandomGen Hub tools run entirely in your browser. Results generated by the random generators stay on your device and are never sent to us or stored on a server. We do not ask you to create an account.

Cookies and advertising

We use Google AdSense to display ads, which keeps the Site free. Google and its partners use cookies to serve personalised ads.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising at Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under the GDPR, UK GDPR, or Brazil’s LGPD. Because the Site does not hold personal data about you directly, most requests relate to the advertising cookies above.

Children

The Site is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy at any time. The “last updated” date above will reflect any changes.
