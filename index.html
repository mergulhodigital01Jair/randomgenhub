About RandomGen Hub

RandomGen Hub is a free collection of online random generators designed to save you time. Whether you're looking for a fantasy name for your RPG character, a secure password for a new account, or a fun team name for your next game night — we've got you covered.

All generators run entirely in your browser. We don't store any of your inputs or generated results. No account, no email, no tracking.

Fantasy Name Generator

Our fantasy name generator creates authentic-sounding names for elves, warriors, wizards, dwarves, dragons, and orcs. Perfect for D&D campaigns, tabletop RPGs, fantasy writing, video games, and any creative project that needs a memorable character name.

Password Generator

Creating a strong password is one of the most important things you can do to protect your online accounts. Our password generator lets you customize length, character types, and complexity to match any website's requirements. Generated passwords never leave your browser.

Username Generator

Finding a username that's both unique and available can be frustrating. Our username generator offers five styles — gaming, cool, funny, dark/edgy, and cute — so you can find something that matches your personality for any platform.

Team Name Generator

From sports leagues to office trivia nights to esports tournaments, a great team name sets the tone. Our team name generator covers sports, gaming, workplace, quiz, and funny categories with hundreds of creative combinations.

Random Number Generator

Need a truly random number? Whether it's for a lottery, a game, statistical sampling, or a classroom activity, our random number generator gives you instant results between any range you define.

Dragon Name Generator

Dragons deserve powerful names. Our dragon name generator creates epic, unique names inspired by fire, ice, shadow, and storm — perfect for Dungeons & Dragons, fantasy novels, or any world-building project.
